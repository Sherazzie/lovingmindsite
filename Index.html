Welcome to Loving Mind

Loving Mind is an organisation that serves with a heart. Individuals and families undergo structured programmes and counselling to lead better lives.
Our counsellors are also trained to equip caregivers with skills to manage their emotions and stress in order to improve the lives of caregivers and their loved ones.
Founded in late 2019, our counsellors provide a safe environment for our clients seeking support.
We provide empathetic and non-judgemental support to individuals, families, couples, children, teenagers, and caregivers.
Loving Mind also offers workshops such as parenting seminars, support group for caregivers, and pre-marital workshops.
